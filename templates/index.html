OPERATIONS

Keeping It Clear and Simple, Yet Profound.

Step 1: Open Camera

Begin by opening your camera to capture your sign language gestures. Our system will continuously analyze the video feed to recognize your signs and facilitate smooth communication.

Step 2: Video Data Transfer

Your camera feed is seamlessly transferred to our server, frame by frame, ensuring every subtle gesture is captured accurately. This real-time data exchange is the first step in our sign language translation process.

Step 3: Translation & Feedback

Our server processes every 40 frames, uses a pre-trained model to recognize sign language with a confidence level above 0.5, and sends the corresponding English text as feedback.
